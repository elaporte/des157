ISEA 2017 Topic Choice

My project will be an app for user input reports about local sexual violence, threats, personal stories, and more. The idea is to send push notifications for those within a certain radius to protect the community, promote the buddy system and encourage awareness to your surroundings. It will include a map, a newsfeed and a user profile to allow users to choose their radius, the locations they want to know about and a friend's list. The friend's list will introduce users to others in their neighborhood and create a growing community and a new network for people to call for safety.
